HIV.B.18

title: Provide voluntary partner services

description:

Partner services include partner notification, contact tracing, index testing and family-based index case testing for reaching partners of people with HIV. In this kit “partner services” is used as an inclusive term encompassing a range of partner services packages and approaches, including social network-based approaches. HIV partner services can be delivered in many ways, including patient referral and provider-assisted referral.
